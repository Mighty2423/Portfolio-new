About Me

Hello, my name is Alton White. I was born in Auburn on February 15, 1999. Raised in a military family, I moved ten times and have seen all of the U.S. except Alaska and California. As an Air Force brat, I matured quickly in some areas and missed opportunities in others. I’m the eldest of four children and deeply value my parents’ support and influence.

My hobbies include plastic model building, exercising, drawing, tabletop and video gaming. I play the trombone and trumpet, and I’m a fan of anime and K-dramas. My dad and I built models together since I was four, and I’ve grown to love building Gundam from mecha anime. I’m also into Warhammer 40K, Magic the Gathering, and Marvel/DC comics. I enjoy drawing in a style influenced by manga and anime.

I achieved my Eagle Scout award in 2016 after digitizing nearly 80 pages of logs for the North Hampton Historical Society. It taught me a lot about delegation and time management. I hold an associate degree in Computer Network Engineering & Cybersecurity from Pierce College and am currently pursuing a bachelor's in CIT.

My personal motto: “The only thing you can change about the past is how you feel about it in the present.” – Yōsuke Kuroda
